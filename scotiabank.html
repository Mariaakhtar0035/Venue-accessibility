Scotiabank Arena

Accessible Seating

  • Small balcony sections without fixed seats (100, 300, 600 levels)
  • Regular seats are stair access exclusively
  • Some guests recommend the lower level accessible seating if possible (100’s) as it is less restricted (600’s can be obstructed)
  • Do not buy standing ticket GA because visibility will be limited and you may be asked to move towards the back of the area

Restrooms

  • All washrooms wheelchair accessible

Ticket Information

  • Up to 4 seats may be purchased to accommodate the accessible needs fan, a support person and/or guests
  • Call # for inquiries: (855) 682-6736 to book accessible seating (or Ticketmaster "W” class seats)

Accessible Parking

  • 4 onsite accessible parking spaces (416) 815-5982 (reserve up to 4 weeks in advance, reservations are required and early is good)

Accessible Entrance

  • Accessible entrance: gate 1 galleria, wheelchair escort if needed, notify the front desk after arrival
  • Best dropoff point is west side gate 5/6 area
